Straight Answers

Things people ask before booking.

Q.01What if my garage is bigger than 2 cars?

That's Tier 3 territory. We do a free on-site walkthrough, assess the full scope, and give you a firm flat price before any work starts. Text us a photo — we'll give you a ballpark within 2 hours. No hourly surprises, ever.

Q.02What's the catch with "pay 50% less"?

No catch. We're building reputation from zero and cannot afford a bad job. If our work is good enough that you'd leave an honest Google review, we charge full launch price. If we didn't earn it, we eat half. That incentive is entirely on us.

Q.03What do you NOT haul?

Hazardous materials only: asbestos, live paint, motor oil in quantity, medical waste. We flag these during the quote. Everything else — appliances, furniture, boxes, tires, yard equipment, mattresses — fair game.

Q.04Do I need to be home?

Yes at the start — 20-30 minutes to walk through what goes and what stays. After that we work unsupervised. Most customers run errands. We text photos before anything big goes in the truck and you can reach us directly the whole time.

Q.05How does the tax receipt actually work?

We itemize every donated item — furniture, appliances, boxes, clothing, tools — and get you a receipt from the charity. You give that to your CPA. Most 2-car garage cleanouts generate $200–500 in deductible donations. It typically nets your real out-of-pocket cost well below the sticker price.

Q.06Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Easy Garage Cleaning LLC is a Colorado-registered limited liability company with general liability and commercial auto insurance. Fort Collins city business license on file. Proof of insurance available on request before we show up.

Q.07How do I pay?

On-site when we finish — tap-to-pay (Apple Pay, Google Pay, any card). Receipt sent immediately. If you prefer a deposit first and final payment on completion, we can do that too.

Straight Answers

Questions people ask before booking.

Q.01What does "done by dinner" actually mean?

It means we finish your cleanout by 6 PM the day we start — usually earlier. If we book your job, we don't leave until it's done. If for any reason we can't finish by end of day, the entire job is free. No partial credit, no "we'll come back tomorrow at half price." Free.

Q.02Who actually does the work?

Easy Garage Cleaning is a locally owned and operated Colorado LLC. The owners are on every job — we're not a franchise, not a call center, not a national brand sending whoever's available. You'll know the names and faces of the people in your garage before they show up.

Q.03Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Easy Garage Cleaning LLC is a Colorado-registered limited liability company with general liability and commercial auto insurance. Fort Collins city business license on file. Proof of insurance available on request before we show up.

Q.04Why flat rate instead of hourly?

Because hourly creates the wrong incentive. You'd be watching the clock, wondering if we're moving fast enough, never sure what the final bill will be. We quote a firm price before we start — even if it takes us longer than expected, the price doesn't move.

Q.05What if my garage is bigger than 2 cars?

That's Tier 3 territory. We do a free on-site walkthrough, assess the full scope, and give you a firm flat price before any work starts. Text us a photo and we'll give you a ballpark within 2 hours.

Q.06What do you NOT haul?

Hazardous materials only: asbestos, live paint, motor oil in quantity, medical waste, ammunition. We flag these during the quote. Everything else — appliances, furniture, boxes, tires, yard equipment, mattresses, old electronics — fair game.

Q.07Do I need to be home all day?

Just the first 20 minutes — the walkthrough where you tell us what stays and what goes. After that, most customers run errands, go to lunch, or work from home. We text photos before anything big leaves and you can reach us directly the entire time.

Q.08How does the donation receipt work?

We deliver usable items to Habitat ReStore, ARC, and Goodwill and bring you an itemized receipt. Whether it helps on your taxes depends on your situation — ask your CPA. Either way, your stuff goes to a good home instead of a landfill.

Q.09What areas do you serve?

Fort Collins, Loveland, Windsor, and Timnath only. We don't service Greeley, Cheyenne, Denver metro, or anywhere outside this area — we keep it tight so we can stay accountable to local neighbors and respond same-day.

Q.10How do I pay?

On-site when we finish — tap-to-pay (Apple Pay, Google Pay, any card). Receipt sent to your email immediately.

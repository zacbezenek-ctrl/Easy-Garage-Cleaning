05 — FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

Q.01How much does a garage cleanout cost in Fort Collins?

We offer flat-rate, all-inclusive pricing — no per-item fees or surprise charges. Get your free quote in under 24 hours by filling out the form below or calling us directly. Larger jobs, estates, and custom cleanouts are quoted after a free on-site walkthrough.

Q.02What do you haul away?

Almost anything: old furniture, appliances, water heaters, tires, boxes, tools, yard equipment, construction debris, and general household junk. We can't haul hazardous materials like asbestos, live paint, or medical waste, but we'll let you know if anything falls outside our scope during the quote.

Q.03Do you actually donate items?

Yes. Usable items — furniture, working appliances, household goods — go to local Fort Collins charities including Habitat for Humanity ReStore, ARC Thrift, and Goodwill. We provide you with a donation receipt in your name so you can claim the deduction on your taxes.

Q.04How fast can you come out?

We respond to every quote request within 5 minutes and offer same-day service when our schedule allows. Most jobs are booked within 2–3 days of the initial quote.

Q.05Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Easy Garage Cleaning LLC is a Colorado-registered limited liability company carrying general liability and commercial auto insurance. We're licensed to operate in the City of Fort Collins.

Q.06What if I'm not sure what I want to keep?

No problem. We work alongside you — you point, we haul. If you're undecided on something, we set it aside for you to review before it leaves the garage. No pressure, no rush.
